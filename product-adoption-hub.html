Deep Dive

Adoption Across the Journey

What the Product Adoption team does at each phase, who they work with, and what they measure. Every task maps back to the customer journey.

01
Awareness

Adoption's Role

Shape the narrative of what "success" looks like. Provide Marketing with real usage stories, activation benchmarks, and proof points that make the product tangible to prospects.

Key Tasks

Supply adoption case studies and usage data for marketing content. Define the "promise" of time-to-value that Marketing can communicate. Co-author customer success webinars.

Collaborators

Product Marketing StratOps

Key Metric

Content engagement rate on adoption-sourced materials

02
Evaluation

Adoption's Role

Reduce perceived risk. Arm Field Engineering and FDEs with onboarding previews, sandbox environments, and guided trial experiences that let prospects feel the value before they buy.

Key Tasks

Build self-serve trial onboarding flows. Create "day 1 to day 30" journey maps for POC evaluations. Provide Field Engineering with adoption readiness assessments.

Collaborators

Field Engineering FDEs Product Marketing

Key Metric

Trial-to-paid conversion rate

03
Onboarding

Adoption's Role

Primary owner. Orchestrate the first 30 days to compress time-to-first-value. Design, measure, and iterate on every touchpoint between purchase and first meaningful use.

Key Tasks

Design guided onboarding sequences (in-app tours, checklists, email drips). Coordinate with SIs on implementation handoff standards. Set activation milestones and track progress. Triage at-risk accounts with Field Engineering.

Collaborators

FDEs SIs Field Engineering Product Management

Key Metric

Time-to-first-value (TTFV) — median days to activation milestone

04
Activation

Adoption's Role

Primary owner. Ensure the customer crosses the "aha moment" threshold and embeds the product into their workflow. This is where retention is won or lost.

Key Tasks

Monitor activation cohorts and intervene on stalled accounts. Run adoption campaigns for underused features. Feed friction patterns back to Product Management for roadmap input. Partner with FDEs to create repeatable success playbooks.

Collaborators

Product Management FDEs StratOps

Key Metric

DAU/MAU ratio — depth of engagement after activation

05
Expansion

Adoption's Role

Primary owner. Turn healthy adoption into growth. Identify expansion signals (usage hitting limits, new use-case exploration) and coordinate with commercial teams to capture that intent.

Key Tasks

Build expansion scoring models from usage data. Launch in-app discovery of premium features. Coordinate with SIs on add-on implementation. Provide Marketing with expansion narratives for upsell campaigns. Surface product feedback to PM on what customers need next.

Collaborators

SIs Product Marketing FDEs Product Management

Key Metric

Net Revenue Retention (NRR) — expansion minus churn

06
Advocacy

Adoption's Role

Cultivate champions. Identify power users and high-NPS accounts, then partner with Marketing and FDEs to convert their success into referenceable stories that feed back into Phase 01.

Key Tasks

Flag advocacy-ready accounts based on usage and satisfaction scores. Facilitate customer advisory boards and beta programs. Co-produce case studies with Marketing. Connect champions to the FDE network for community building.

Collaborators

Product Marketing FDEs Product Management

Key Metric

Customer reference-ability score & NPS

Interactions

How Adoption Connects to Each Team

The bidirectional exchanges between Product Adoption and every other function, mapped to concrete deliverables.

Product Management

Strategic partner
→ Adoption sends friction data, feature adoption rates, and churn signals to influence roadmap prioritization
← PM sends feature specs, release timelines, and onboarding requirements for new capabilities

Product StratOps

Data backbone
→ Adoption sends usage funnels, activation cohort data, and retention metrics for cross-org reporting
← StratOps sends segment benchmarks, competitive adoption comparisons, and funnel analytics

Product Marketing

Narrative partner
→ Adoption sends success stories, usage proof points, and champion profiles for content and campaigns
← Marketing sends lifecycle campaign assets, positioning updates, and expansion messaging

Field Engineering

Frontline collaborator
→ Adoption sends health scores, at-risk alerts, and intervention playbooks for proactive account management
← Field Eng sends customer health signals, technical blockers, and post-sale onboarding context

Forward Deployed Engineers

Ground truth source
→ Adoption sends adoption gap analysis and at-risk strategic accounts needing embedded support
← FDEs send deployment playbooks, advanced integration patterns, and deep-dive engagement data

Systems Integrators

Scale multiplier
→ Adoption sends enablement standards, success criteria for SI-led deployments, and QA benchmarks
← SIs send implementation completion data, post-deploy handoff status, and customer readiness signals
